在我的Debian上安装三星710N显示器

May 16th, 2005

一直以为在linux上配置LCD显示器跟CRT显示器有很大的不同.今天试了下,发现其实都是一样的,只是LCD对刷新率跟分辨率比较敏感.

我用的系统是Hiweed 0.6RC2+GNOME2.8

显卡是NVIDIA Geforce2 MX200 没有装NVIDIA的驱动 只是用XFree86自带的.

因为以前机器上有一个15的显示器,对应的XF86Config-4文件是这样的:

Section “Device”

Identifier “Geforce2 MX200”

Driver “nv”

EndSection

Section “Monitor”

Identifier “Samsung 510B”

HorizSync 45-50

VertRefresh 60-72

Option “DPMS”

EndSection

Section “Screen”

Identifier “Default Screen”

Device “Geforce2 MX200”

Monitor “Samsung 510B”

DefaultDepth 24

SubSection “Display”

Depth 1

Modes “1152x864” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 4

Modes “1152x864” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 8

Modes “1152x864” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 15

Modes “1152x864” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 16

Modes “1152x864” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 24

Modes “1152x864” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

EndSection

把710N装上后 用这个配置文件显示字体有轻微的发虚,于是我试着改水平跟垂直刷新率.

但是改完刷新率(自己乱改的 – – )后,字体明显的发虚, 就象在RH9刚刚安装完那种感觉.特别难受.

接着找了下710N的手册 发现它的水平跟垂直刷新率是这样的:

Section “Monitor”

Identifier “Samsung 710N”

HorizSync 30-81

VertRefresh 56-75

Option “DPMS”

EndSection

然后把分辨率也提高,它推荐的分辨率是1280x1024.

Section “Screen”

Identifier “Default Screen”

Device “Geforce2 MX200”

Monitor “Samsung 710N”

DefaultDepth 24

SubSection “Display”

Depth 1

Modes “1280x1024” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 4

Modes “1280x1024” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 8

Modes “1280x1024” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 15

Modes “1280x1024” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 16

Modes “1280x1024” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

SubSection “Display”

Depth 24

Modes “1280x1024” “1024x768” “800x600” “640x480”

EndSubSection

EndSection

改完了 重启X. 清晰锐利的字体就呈现在眼前了.绝对不比在windows里的效果差.哈哈~
